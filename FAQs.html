FAQs

What services do you offer? +

We offer residential and commercial cleaning, including regular cleaning, deep cleaning, and specialized services like carpet cleaning and window washing.

How do I schedule a cleaning? +

You can schedule a cleaning through our online booking system, or by calling our customer service team. We offer flexible times to accommodate your schedule.

Are your cleaners insured? +

Yes, all of our cleaners are fully insured and undergo background checks to ensure the highest level of trust and security.

How much does cleaning cost? +

Our pricing depends on the size of the property and the type of service required. You can get an estimate directly on our website or contact us for a personalized quote.

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies? +

No, we provide all the necessary cleaning supplies. However, if you have specific preferences, feel free to let us know in advance.

What if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning? +

If you're not completely satisfied with the cleaning, please contact us within 24 hours and we'll send a team back to re-clean the areas in question.
